Welcome

Welcome to my academic website. I am Dr. Peikai Li, a Lecturer in Organizational Behavior at the University of Leeds. My research interests include leadership, work stress, employee well-being, and advanced statistical methodologies.

Research

I conduct research in the field of organizational behavior, focusing on leadership at individual, team, and country levels, stress management, workplace well-being, employee creativity, and newcomer socialization.

Research Methods & Skills

My methodological expertise includes advanced meta-analysis (multilevel meta-analysis, meta-mediation analysis), structural equation modeling (SEM), multi-level and longitudinal data analysis with proficiency in Mplus, R, and SPSS.
