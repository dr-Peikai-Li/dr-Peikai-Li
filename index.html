Research Overview

My research focuses on organizational behavior, including leadership at multiple levels, stress management, creativity, newcomer socialization, and workplace well-being. I employ advanced methods such as meta-analysis, SEM, and multilevel/longitudinal analyses using R, Mplus, and SPSS.

Teaching & Supervision

I teach courses in Organizational Behavior, Leadership, and Research Methods. I have supervised over 20 Master's students and regularly conduct workshops on meta-analysis, Mplus, and data analysis.
